# Code of Conduct

OpenScrobbler should be a focused, respectful place to build open music tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming technical risks clearly.
- Keep feedback about code, behavior, design, documentation, and project direction.
- Respect that people have different levels of macOS, Swift, MusicBrainz, and ListenBrainz experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers ask to refocus.
- Dismissing bug reports without investigation or a clear technical reason.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent blocking from the project.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact a maintainer through GitHub. Include relevant links and enough context for maintainers to understand what happened.
